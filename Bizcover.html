About The insurance

BizCover is an online platform that provides insurance solutions for small and medium-sized businesses in Australia. The platform offers a range of insurance products, including:

  • 1. Public Liability Insurance: This covers businesses against claims of property damage or personal injury caused by their business operations.
  • 2. Professional Indemnity Insurance: This covers businesses against claims of negligence or breach of duty arising from their professional services or advice.
  • 3. Business Insurance: This provides cover for a range of business risks, including property damage, theft, and loss of income due to business interruption.
  • 4. Cyber Insurance: This provides protection against cyber threats such as hacking, data breaches, and cyber extortion.
  • 5. Management Liability Insurance: This provides cover for directors and officers of a company against claims of wrongful acts, including employment practices liability and statutory liability.

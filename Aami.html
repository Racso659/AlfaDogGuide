About The Insurance

AAMI (Australian Associated Motor Insurers Limited) is an insurance company in Australia that primarily provides car insurance, as well as other types of insurance products such as home insurance, travel insurance, life insurance, and business insurance.

If you need to report a problem that needs to be fixed with your insurance, there are several ways you can do this:

Contact AAMI

If you need to report a problem with your AAMI insurance, there are several ways to get in touch:

Online

You can log in to your AAMI account online and report the issue by filling out the contact form. This form allows you to provide details of the problem you are experiencing, including any relevant policy numbers and dates. AAMI will typically respond to your inquiry within a few business days.

Phone

You can call AAMI's customer service line at 13 22 44 (within Australia) to speak with a representative about your issue. They will

Email

You can also email AAMI's customer service team at customerservice@aami.com.au to report the problem. Be sure to provide as much detail as possible about the issue you are experiencing and include any relevant policy numbers and dates.

In Person

If you prefer to speak with someone in person, you can visit an AAMI store or one of their partner stores. AAMI's website has a store locator tool that can help you find the nearest location to you.
